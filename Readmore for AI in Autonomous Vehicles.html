Artificial Intelligence in Autonomous Vehicles

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has revolutionized the automotive industry, enabling vehicles to operate without human intervention. By integrating advanced technologies like machine learning, computer vision, and neural networks, self-driving cars can analyze their environment in real time.

Key features include traffic prediction, pedestrian detection, and adaptive control systems. These advancements not only enhance road safety but also pave the way for efficient and eco-friendly transportation solutions.
